### Q1
Answer: 25%
Explanation: Let the CP of 1 article be 1. Then CP of 15 articles = 15. The SP of 12 articles = CP of 15 articles = 15. Therefore, SP of 1 article = 15/12 = 1.25. The gain on 1 article is 1.25 - 1 = 0.25. Gain percent = (0.25 / 1) * 100 = 25%.

### Q2
Answer: 6.67 days
Explanation: A's 1-day work = 1/12; B's 1-day work = 1/15. Working together, their 1-day work = 1/12 + 1/15 = 5/60 + 4/60 = 9/60 = 3/20. Total days to finish the work = 20/3 = 6.67 days.

### Q3
Answer: Rs 1250
Explanation: The formula for compound interest is A = P(1 + r/100)^n. Substituting the given values: 1352 = P * (1 + 4/100)^2 => 1352 = P * (1.04)^2 => 1352 = P * 1.0816. Therefore, P = 1352 / 1.0816 = Rs 1250.

### Q4
Answer: 45 seconds
Explanation: Speed of the train = Length / Time = 150 / 15 = 10 m/s. The total distance to cover when passing a platform is the length of the train + length of the platform = 150 + 300 = 450 m. Time taken = Total distance / Speed = 450 / 10 = 45 seconds.

### Q5

### Q12
Answer: 33

### Q13

### Q14

### Q15

### Q1
Answer: 32 litres
Explanation: Let milk = 3x and water = x. After adding 8 litres of water, the new equation is 3x / (x + 8) = 3 / 2. Cross-multiplying gives 6x = 3x + 24, which simplifies to 3x = 24, so x = 8. The original quantity of the mixture is 3x + x = 4x. Therefore, 4 * 8 = 32 litres.
